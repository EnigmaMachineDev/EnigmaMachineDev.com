Abundance

Notice what's good. A quiet daily journal for logging what you're grateful for and reflecting on your day — building a record of the things that matter.

Coming to Google Play Privacy Policy
Features

What Abundance does

🌱

Daily Gratefuls

Log a few things you're grateful for each day. A simple, low-friction prompt that takes thirty seconds — and compounds over time into something meaningful.

✍️

Daily Reflection

Add a short reflection alongside your gratefuls. Write as little or as much as you want — a single sentence or a full recap of the day.

📅

Journal View

Browse your full history of entries in a clean chronological log. Read back through your days and notice how your perspective has shifted.

📈

Insights & Analysis

See your streaks and the words and themes that recur across your entries — a quiet reflection on what you keep coming back to.

🔔

Gentle Reminders

Set one or more daily notification times to prompt your entry. Snooze or silence reminders on days you don't need them — no guilt, no streaks pressure.
