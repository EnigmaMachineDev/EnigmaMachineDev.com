Artisan

Know what your work is really worth. A workshop ledger for makers — track each piece as you build it, tally materials and labor, set a price that respects your time, and follow it through to the sale.

Coming to Google Play Privacy Policy
Features

What Artisan does

🪵

Project Tracking

Follow every piece through its full lifecycle — in progress, finished inventory, and sold. Your whole workshop, organized in one ledger.

🧾

Material Costs

Log every material and expense that goes into a build, with categories and notes. Costs accumulate as the project grows so you always know what you've put in.

⏱️

Time & Labor Tracking

Record the hours you spend on each piece at your chosen hourly rate. Artisan folds labor into the true cost so your pricing reflects your time, not just supplies.

🏷️

Smart Pricing

Price by project type using the strategy that fits — a cost multiplier, cost plus a flat margin, or a fixed target price — with selling-platform fees factored in.

📷

Project Photos

Attach photos of each piece straight from your camera or gallery. Images are compressed and stored privately on your device.

📊

Sales & Insights

See profit, ROI, sell-through, and average time to sell across your work. Export your full ledger as a ZIP or CSV to keep your records wherever you want.
