Attune

Find the foods that don't agree with you. Log your meals, check in on how you feel a while later, and let Attune surface the patterns between what you eat and how your body responds.

Coming to Google Play Privacy Policy
Features

What Attune does

🍽️

Meal Logging

Log meals from a personal food library with tags and a light/medium/heavy portion. Add new foods on the fly, and tack on a snack to the meal you just ate.

⏱️

Timed Check-ins

Choose how long after eating Attune should prompt you — one reminder or several. When it fires, record your overall feel and anything you're noticing.

🩺

Symptom Tracking

A full, customizable symptom list spanning digestion, skin, energy, mood and more. Rate severity, toggle what you track, or add your own symptoms.

📊

Food Insights

Foods and tags ranked by how they make you feel — worst offenders, best vibes, symptom timelines, time-of-day patterns, and recent suspects to cut.

🔔

Smart Reminders

Per-meal local notifications that deep-link straight to your check-in. Silence them for a few hours whenever you need a break — no pressure.

🔒

Yours & Private

Everything stays on your device. Export a full backup or import it onto a new phone whenever you like — no account, no cloud, no tracking.
