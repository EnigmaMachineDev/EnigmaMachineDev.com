Android · Fermentation Tracker

Brine

Never miss a burp, taste, or rack again. Start a ferment, attach the steps it needs — scheduled relative to the day you began — and Brine rolls everything that's due into a single Today view with its own reminders. Built-in templates for sauerkraut, kombucha, sourdough, kimchi, hot sauce, and ale get you going in seconds, while gently reminding you to judge readiness by taste and smell, not just the calendar.

Features

What Brine does

⏱️

Relative Scheduling

Every action is timed off the ferment's start date in whole days — "burp daily for two weeks", "taste at day 7", "rack at day 14". One-time or repeating, with three ways to end a repeat.

✅

Today View

Everything due today and anything overdue, gathered across all your active ferments. Check items off as you go, or skip what you don't need.

🔔

Per-Action Reminders

Each action carries its own reminder time. Local notifications fire right on schedule — no account and no connection required.

🧪

Starter Templates

Sauerkraut, kombucha, sourdough starter, kimchi, hot sauce mash, and extract ale come ready to apply — fully editable, and honest that real fermentation is judged by the senses.

📈

Measurements

Optionally log temperature, pH, and specific gravity over a ferment's life to track its progress alongside your scheduled steps.

🔒

Yours & Private

Everything stays on your device. Export a full JSON backup or import one on a new phone whenever you like — no cloud, no tracking.
