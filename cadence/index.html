Cadence

Task management with rhythm. Organize your work by type, track progress your way, and cut through decision paralysis with a focused daily block.

Coming to Google Play Privacy Policy
Features

What Cadence does

📁

Task Type Organization

Create custom task categories and navigate between them from a dedicated drawer menu that puts every type one tap away.

🏷️

Custom Statuses & Flags

Define your own status labels and flag types to track exactly how tasks are progressing — no predefined workflows imposed on you.

🎲

Daily Block Generator

Generate a randomized, focused to-do list from your pending tasks. Break the cycle of staring at a backlog and not knowing where to start.

🔄

Import & Export

Back up your entire task database as a JSON file and restore it on any device. Your data is always yours to take with you.

📵

Offline First

Works entirely without an internet connection. No sync, no account required — data lives on your device and nowhere else.

🚀

Lightweight & Fast

No subscriptions, no cloud accounts, no tracking. A focused tool that opens instantly and gets out of your way.
