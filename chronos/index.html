Android · Time Banking

Chronos

Budget your time like money. Create time banks, divide a pool of hours across the activities that matter, then track each one with a stopwatch. Spend past an allocation and it goes into the red — surplus and debt roll over from one cycle to the next.

Coming to Google Play Privacy Policy
Features

What Chronos does

🏦

Time Banks

Group your activities into banks. Allocate by absolute hours and minutes, or set a total budget and split it by percentage — Chronos warns you, but never blocks you, when you over-allocate.

⏱️

Wall-Clock Stopwatch

Track time against any activity with a stopwatch that counts down or up. It's anchored to real time, so it survives backgrounding, pausing, and even the app being killed.

📉

Time Debt

Overrun an allocation and the balance goes negative. Activities deplete independently, and you can keep digging into the red on purpose when a task just needs more time.

🔄

Cycles & Rollover

When every activity is used up, the cycle refreshes — automatically or on a prompt. Choose whether leftover surplus and accrued debt carry forward, per activity.

🔔

Overage Alerts

Get a local notification the moment an activity runs out, even in the background, plus an in-app alert with a silence control so a long session never sneaks past you.

🔒

Yours & Private

Everything stays on your device. Export a full backup or import it onto a new phone whenever you like — no account, no cloud, no tracking.
