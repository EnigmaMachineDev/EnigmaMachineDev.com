Android · First Foods Log

Nourish

A simple log for your baby's first foods. Track which foods have been introduced, when, and how your little one reacted. Nourish is purely a logging tool — it ships with zero food knowledge, makes no medical claims, and never enforces a schedule. Your pediatrician decides; you record.

Coming to Google Play Privacy Policy
Features

What Nourish does

📋

Food Journal

Add foods, mark them as planned, introduced, or shelved. Record the date of each introduction and attach observations with your own tags.

⏳

Wait Window

Set a configurable wait period between introductions. Nourish counts down and notifies you when the window ends — but never blocks or warns.

🏷️

Custom Tags

Create your own food categories and observation labels. Nourish ships empty — every tag is yours, nothing is built in.

📅

History

Browse introductions on a calendar or scroll a timeline. See what was introduced when, and review observations at a glance.

📊

Stats

Purely descriptive numbers: how many foods introduced, your pace, configured-wait-vs-actual gaps, and tag breakdowns.

🔒

Yours & Private

Everything stays on your device. Export a full JSON backup or a CSV of foods and observations whenever you like — no cloud, no tracking.
