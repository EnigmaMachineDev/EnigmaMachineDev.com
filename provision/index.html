Android · Food Inventory

Provision

Keep your kitchen inventory, shopping list, and ingredient preps in one place. Track what you have on hand — by count or as a simple available / unavailable staple — and Provision surfaces what's running low. Build reusable prep templates and roll a random meal from whatever is actually in stock. Fully offline, no account, no tracking.

Features

What Provision does

📦

Inventory

Add ingredients to your own categories. Track each by whole count with quick +/− controls, or as a simple available / unavailable staple.

🛒

Shopping List

Anything you keep in stock appears automatically when it runs low or goes out. Check it off to restock, and add one-off items by hand.

🎲

Ingredient Preps

Build meal templates from category slots — 2 proteins, 2 vegetables, a base — then roll each slot from what's in stock, avoiding repeats.

🏷️

Custom Categories

An ingredient can belong to several categories at once. Rename, recolor, reorder, or delete any of them — the starters are just a head start.

🔁

Smart Restock

Set a low-stock threshold and how many units to add back when purchased. Rolling a prep deducts from counts; staples are never used up.

🔒

Yours & Private

Everything stays on your device in one file. Export a JSON backup or an inventory CSV whenever you like — no cloud, no tracking.
