Features

What RandomizerTools does

🎮

Game Randomizers

Tools for games like Elden Ring — randomize builds, starting classes, or challenge runs. Opinionated options with configurable categories.

🍽️

Food & Activity

Randomizers for meals, restaurants, and activities when the group can't agree. Stop arguing, start spinning.

⚙️

Configurable Options

Toggle categories, exclude specific items, and save your preferences to localStorage so the randomizer remembers your setup.

🌐

No Install Required

Runs entirely in your browser. No downloads, no sign-up, no loading spinner — just open and spin.

📱

Works Everywhere

Fully responsive design works on desktop, tablet, and phone. Same experience no matter the screen size.

🔮

Growing Library

New randomizers are added regularly across different categories. Check back often or follow for updates.
