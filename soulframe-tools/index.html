Features

What SoulframeTools does

⚔️

Weapons Database

Browse every weapon with stats, scaling, and details. Filter and compare to find the right fit for your build.

🛡️

Armour & Equipment

Full armour set listings with defensive values and set bonuses. Plan your loadout before you grind for it.

💍

Runes & Pacts

Reference for rune effects and faction pact requirements. Know exactly what you need before committing to a path.

🔨

Joineries & Prisms

Crafting material reference and joinery browser. Track what you need and where to find it.

✅

Progress Tracker

Check off items as you acquire them. Track completion across weapons, armour, talismans, and totems in one place.

📐

Stat Calculator

Apply game formulas to estimate weapon damage and build effectiveness before you invest your resources.
