StreamDial

Your livestream command center. Curate lists of YouTube and Twitch channels, preview what's live without leaving the page, and hit Randomize to jump into something new. Runs entirely in your browser — no account, no server, no API key.

Open StreamDial
Live at streamdial.net
Features

What StreamDial does

📋

Channel Lists

Organize channels into named lists. One channel can appear in multiple lists. Manage, reorder, and switch between lists instantly.

▶️

Live Preview

Preview a stream right on the page without leaving. One preview active at a time — click another to swap, or close to go back to thumbnails.

🎲

Randomize

Hit Randomize to open a random channel from the current list in a new tab. Optionally avoid picking the same channel twice in a row.

🔗

Paste & Go

Paste a YouTube or Twitch URL and the channel is parsed locally in your browser. No API key, no server round-trip.

💾

Import & Export

Back up your lists as JSON. Move them between browsers or devices. Merge imported data with your existing lists or replace everything.

🔒

Yours & Private

Everything lives in localStorage. No account, no cloud sync, no tracking. Clear your browser data and it's gone — keep a backup on the Data screen.
