Temperance

A neutral mirror for your drinking. Log a drink in a single tap, set optional daily and weekly limits — or skip them entirely — and watch your patterns take shape on a calendar and in your stats. Temperance records the numbers and limits you set; it never recommends a limit or makes a judgment.

Coming to Google Play Privacy Policy
Features

What Temperance does

⚡

One-Tap Logging

The most common action is a single tap. Log a standard drink instantly — and add a title, notes, context tags, or price only when you want to, behind an optional details expander.

🎯

Optional Limits

Set a per-weekday daily limit and a weekly limit, or switch either one off and just track. Limits are yours to define — nothing is ever recommended for you, and a disabled limit simply disappears.

📅

Calendar View

A month grid colors each day against its own limit: alcohol-free, within limit, or over. Tap any day to see the count and review or edit each drink you logged.

📊

Patterns & Stats

Totals, two clearly-labeled averages, drinking and alcohol-free days, dry streaks, and weekday and time-of-day patterns — across 7, 30, 90 days, or all time.

🏷️

Spend & Tags

Add an optional price to see what you spend, and tag drinks with your own context labels — social, home, dinner — to understand what's behind the numbers.

🔒

Yours & Private

Everything stays on your device. Export a full backup or a CSV, import onto a new phone whenever you like — no account, no cloud, no tracking.
