Android · Gift Tracker

Tidings

Never forget a gift day again. Keep a list of occasions — holidays, birthdays, anniversaries — attach the people you buy for, and track each gift from first idea through purchased, wrapped, and given. Budgets are informational, dates are computed, and everything stays on your phone.

Coming to Google Play Privacy Policy
Features

What Tidings does

🎄

Occasions

Holidays, birthdays, and custom dates. Shared occasions have one date for everyone; per-person occasions (like birthdays) let each person have their own.

🎁

Gift Pipeline

Track each gift through five stages: considering, planned, purchased, wrapped, given. See at a glance what's done and what's still to do.

📅

Smart Dates

Thanksgiving, Mother's Day, Easter, and other movable holidays are computed automatically. Fixed dates and manual-per-year entries too.

💰

Budget View

Set a per-person or per-occasion budget. See spend vs. budget at every level — never a hard block, just visibility.

🔔

Reminders

Lead-day and morning-of notifications for upcoming gift days, with readiness-aware copy so you know what's still outstanding.

🔒

Yours & Private

Everything stays on your device. Export a full JSON backup or a flat gifts/spend CSV whenever you like — no cloud, no tracking.
