Features

What Vigil does

🛡️

Heavy Sleeper Mode

Dismiss alarms by typing a randomly generated 20-character code. No fumble-and-snooze, no muscle memory shortcuts — just real accountability.

📂

Alarm Presets

Group alarms into named presets and switch between them instantly — weekday, weekend, travel, or any routine you need.

⚡

Automation Rules

Define conditions — day of week, flags, time windows — that automatically trigger actions like adding alarms, switching presets, or sending notifications.

⏱️

Timers & Stopwatches

Built-in timing tools alongside your alarms so everything lives in one place, without switching between apps.

📅

Week-ahead Scheduling

On launch, Vigil schedules alarms for the full coming week. They'll fire reliably even if you never open the app again.

🔋

Background Reliability

Alarms are automatically rescheduled in the background after device restarts, ensuring nothing gets missed between sessions.
