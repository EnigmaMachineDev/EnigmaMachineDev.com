Yield

Track your flips, maximize your returns. A clean ledger for resellers — log items, evaluate condition, calculate margins, and stay on top of your inventory.

Features

What Yield does

📦

Inventory Management

Log every item you're sourcing or considering for resale with details, notes, and current status — all in one organized ledger.

🔍

Condition Evaluations

Record structured assessments for each item: condition grade, purchase price, target list price, and evaluation notes. Make informed decisions before you commit.

📊

Insights Dashboard

See your full portfolio at a glance — total capital invested, potential returns, and margin estimates across your active inventory.

🎯

Prospect Queue

Track items you're watching or planning to evaluate next. Keep your pipeline organized without cluttering your active inventory.

💰

Pricing Calculator

Calculate estimated profit margins and returns on any item before you buy — factor in acquisition cost, fees, and target price.

📤

Data Portability

Export your full ledger as a JSON file and import it on another device. Your resale history goes wherever you go.
