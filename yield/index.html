Yield

Track your flips, maximize your returns. A clean ledger for resellers — move items from prospect to sold, log costs and platform fees, calculate margins, and stay on top of your inventory.

Coming to Google Play Privacy Policy
Features

What Yield does

📦

Inventory & Lifecycle

Follow every item through its full lifecycle — prospect, active inventory, and sold. Source, list, and sell all from one organized ledger.

🏷️

Item Types & Pricing Rules

Define item types with retail price, buy and sell percentages, and a minimum accepted profit, so Yield can suggest what to pay and what to list at.

💰

Platform Fees

Model the marketplaces you sell on with percentage and flat per-order fees, so your profit and margin estimates reflect what you actually take home.

🧾

Costs & Price History

Add extra costs to any item and track every listing-price change with a reason, so your cost basis and pricing decisions stay fully documented.

📷

Item Photos

Attach photos to each item straight from your camera or gallery. Images are compressed and stored privately on your device.

📊

Insights & Export

Track profit, ROI, sell-through, and average days to sell across your flips. Export your full ledger as a ZIP or CSV and import it on another device.
